Background

My journey in AI started with a deep curiosity about how we can replicate cognitive processes in machines. With a background in computer science and mathematics, I specialize in Natural Language Processing and System Design. I believe that the best way to learn is to distill and share knowledge, which is the primary motivation for brute.ai.

Mission

To explore the frontiers of Artificial Intelligence while keeping the human perspective at the core. I aim to build tools and write content that helps both beginners and experts understand the "why" behind the "how".
