Rules of Prediction!
  • Prediction of a match should be made before the start of the match.
  • An absolute correct prediction will result in +5 points.
  • Prediction of correct winning team and correct goal difference will result in +3 points
  • Prediction of the correct winning team will result in +2 points.
  • Write E7 or C7 for the 7th match of Euro and Copa respectively.
  • If you do not have a key, register yourself and you will receive a key.
  • You need to enter your prediction through the form with your own key.
  • Only one submission will be acceptable per match.
